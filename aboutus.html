First Creations

We first starting trying to draw designs up on procreate on an iPad, and then decided to print those images onto tote bags and stickers. The jewellery aspect started when we decided to make earrings for our own personal use, but after getting charms and beads from a local craft shop, we decided to make bracelets and necklaces as well. When we had enough pieces, we decided to sell them on depop and Etsy. Before we knew it we were growing and sending packages internationally.
